# style-picasso

## Prompt
"A master-level modern art portrait of the subject pet, inspired by the bold cubist and line-art periods of Pablo Picasso. Strictly exclude all easels, paintbrushes, and traditional studio props. The background is a minimal, abstract dark obsidian space with sharp, dramatic geometric shadows and planes.

The pet's anatomical features are subtly and elegantly deconstructed into bold geometric planes and strong, fluid black outlines while maintaining its core character and soul. Use a sophisticated, muted palette of earth tones, charcoal, and ochre, with sudden, deliberate pops of primary blue and red. 

The composition is avant-garde and asymmetrical, with the pet's eyes serving as the intense, emotional anchor of the piece. The texture alternates between smooth, flat color planes and areas of heavy, gritty, sand-mixed oil paint. The entire image feels like a museum-grade modern masterpiece that challenges perspective while honoring the pet's spirit on a raw, textured canvas."
