## Key
- #SwirlingCanvas

- #PostImpressionism

## Prompt
"A master-level, high-dynamic-range oil painting of the subject pet, executed in the passionate and energetic post-impressionist style of Vincent van Gogh. Strictly exclude all easels, palettes, and modern studio equipment. The background is a thick, swirling vortex of midnight blue and deep indigo oil paint, mimicking a turbulent starry night sky that merges seamlessly into a dark, textured floor.

The pet is presented in a bold, frontal upright pose, with its form defined by thick, rhythmic, and directional impasto brushstrokes that seem to vibrate with life. A crown of golden sunflowers subtly circles the base of the pet, their petals rendered with heavy, sculpted layers of paint. 

The color palette is an intense, emotional explosion of vibrant chrome yellows, fiery oranges, and deep Prussian blues. The lighting is surreal and internal, with the pet's eyes glowing with an intense, soulful amber light that cuts through the swirling textures. Every inch of the fur is a series of thick, tactile paint ridges that create a 3D relief effect on the digital canvas. The entire image has the raw, physical, and rustic texture of a heavily worked Van Gogh masterpiece, pulsating with movement and light."
