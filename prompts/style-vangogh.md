## Key
- #SwirlingCanvas
- #ThickOilPaint
- #VibrantYellow
- #PostImpressionism

## Prompt
"A masterwork of heavily textured, physically layered paint. A master-level oil painting of the subject pet [1] from the uploaded image, executed in the passionate and energetic post-impressionist style of Vincent van Gogh. Heavy, sculpted impasto oil paint texture. Visible, highly defined brushstrokes that build up layers of physical paint. A clear, gritty canvas grain textured background. Strictly exclude all easels, palettes, and modern studio equipment. The background is a thick, swirling vortex of midnight blue and deep indigo oil paint, mimicking a turbulent starry night sky that merges seamlessly into a dark, textured floor.

The pet is presented in a bold, frontal upright pose. The fur is rendered by heavy, multi-directional, expressive brushstrokes that give it a tactile, 3D relief quality—sculpted with thick paint, not smoothly blended. A crown of golden sunflowers subtly circles the base of the pet, their petals rendered with heavy, sculpted layers of paint. The color palette is an intense, emotional explosion of vibrant chrome yellows, fiery oranges, and deep Prussian blues. The lighting is surreal and internal; light sources are defined by heavy dabs of pure pigment, creating raised ridges of paint, with the pet's eyes glowing with an intense, soulful amber light that cuts through the swirling textures. Every inch of the fur is a series of thick, tactile paint ridges that create a 3D relief effect on the canvas.

Prioritize the raw, physical, tactile quality of paint on a textured canvas, visible even upon close inspection. Generate with a textured canvas finish."
