# style-vermeer

## Concept
- 부드러운 측면광
- 정적이고 온화한 분위기
- 세밀한 직물 묘사

## Key
- #WindowLight
- #SoftTexture
- #VermeerBlue
- #PearlAccents

## Prompt
Create a refined portrait of a pet in the spirit of Vermeer. Use gentle window-side lighting, quiet composition, and calm emotional tone. Prioritize soft gradients on fur, delicate textile detail, and restrained palette centered on muted blue and pearl highlights. Maintain elegant realism with subtle painterly texture, avoiding harsh contrast and modern props.
