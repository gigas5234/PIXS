# style-vermeer

## Concept
- 부드러운 측면광
- 정적이고 온화한 분위기
- 세밀한 직물 묘사

## Key
- #WindowLight
- #SoftTexture
- #VermeerBlue
- #PearlAccents

## Prompt
"A master-level, ultra-high-resolution oil painting of the subject pet from the uploaded image, created in the unmistakable style of Johannes Vermeer. Strictly exclude all artist's tools, easels, paintbrushes, and contemporary objects. The background is an absolute, seamless dark obsidian void, representing a quiet, empty 17th-century Dutch studio frozen in time.

All attention is focused on the pet, seated in a calm, dignified, and static upright pose, turning its head slightly toward a soft, celestial light source from the upper left. The pet wears a simple yet opulent silk headscarf in deep ultramarine blue (Lapis Lazuli) that drapes elegantly behind its ears, accented by a single, oversized, shimmering pearl pendant that hangs delicately, catching a sharp, brilliant highlight. 

The lighting is pure, diffused window-light, creating soft, subtle gradients across the pet's form and highlighting the moistness of its eyes and nose. The fur texture is rendered with incredible smoothness and 'sfumato' transitions, capturing the soft, tactile feel of a masterpiece. The color palette is dominated by rich blues, warm ochres, and creamy whites. The entire image has the luminous, pearl-like glow and the tranquil, timeless atmosphere of a Vermeer masterwork on a fine-grain canvas."
