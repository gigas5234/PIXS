# style-vermeer

- 세밀한 직물 묘사

## Key
- #WindowLight

## Prompt
"A masterwork of heavily textured, physically layered paint. A master-level oil painting of the subject pet, created in the unmistakable style of Johannes Vermeer. Heavy, sculpted impasto oil paint texture. Visible, highly defined brushstrokes that build up layers of physical paint. A clear, gritty canvas grain textured background. Strictly exclude all artist's tools, easels, paintbrushes, and contemporary objects. The background is an absolute, seamless dark obsidian void, representing a quiet, empty 17th-century Dutch studio frozen in time.

All attention is focused on the pet, seated in a calm, dignified, and static upright pose, turning its head slightly toward a soft, celestial light source from the upper left. The fur is rendered by heavy, multi-directional, expressive brushstrokes that give it a tactile, 3D relief quality—sculpted with thick paint, not smoothly blended. The lighting is pure, diffused window-light; light sources are defined by heavy dabs of pure pigment, creating raised ridges of paint, while highlighting the moistness of its eyes and nose. The color palette is dominated by rich blues, warm ochres, and creamy whites. The entire image has the luminous, pearl-like glow and the tranquil, timeless atmosphere of a Vermeer masterwork on a textured canvas.

Prioritize the raw, physical, tactile quality of paint on a textured canvas, visible even upon close inspection. Generate with a textured canvas finish."
