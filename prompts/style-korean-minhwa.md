The color palette is composed of traditional 'Obangsaek' (five cardinal colors), using muted, earthy pigments like indigo, deep red, and ochre. The lighting is flat yet warm, consistent with traditional oriental painting styles. The entire image has the visible, fibrous texture of hand-pressed Hanji paper and the soulful, humorous charm of a centuries-old Korean masterpiece.

Prioritize the raw, physical, tactile quality of brushwork on textured paper, visible even upon close inspection. Generate with a textured canvas finish."
