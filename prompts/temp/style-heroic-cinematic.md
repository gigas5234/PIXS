## Prompt
"A master-level, ultra-high-resolution cinematic studio photograph of the subject pet [1] from the uploaded image, presented as a full-body, heroic portrait. Strictly exclude all historical artist's tools, easels, and classic oil painting textures. The background is a complete, absolute, seamless dark obsidian studio void, reflecting a high-end Hollywood movie set.

All attention is focused on the subject pet, seated in a formal, elegant upright 'heroic' pose, looking directly and confidently at the viewer with its natural eye color intensified and sharp focus. It wears a meticulously designed, high-tech futuristic tactical harness featuring complex, layered metallic plating with a brushed titanium and crimson carbon-fiber finish. A subtle, glowing cyan energy core is integrated into the center of the chest piece, emitting a soft light. This advanced armor is custom-fitted perfectly to the pet's specific anatomy, draping gracefully around its form.

The subject pet sits centered on a clean, subtly textured, dark grey metallic studio platform, which rests on the seamless black void. The lighting is pure, intense cinematic hero-lighting: a powerful cool blue key light from the upper left dramatically illuminates one side of the pet's face and its metallic gear, while a warm red rim light from the right defines its silhouette against the deep shadows.

The fur texture is highly defined, showing every fine detail of the subject pet's coat under professional studio strobe lights. The entire image has the crisp, sharp, and glossy tactile texture of a high-end digital IMAX movie poster, free of any watermarks."
