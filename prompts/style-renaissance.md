## Key
- #RoyalVelvet
- #IntricateLace

## Prompt
Create a Renaissance-inspired formal portrait of a pet with royal wardrobe details. Feature a velvet mantle, intricate lace collar accents, and ornamental gold-thread elements. Use a balanced, symmetrical composition with precise facial rendering and dignified posture. Keep lighting noble and painterly, avoiding contemporary visual clutter.
