# style-dreamy-fairytale

## Concept
- 몽환적인 글로우 조명
- 극도로 부드러운 털 묘사
- 동화적 소품 연출

## Key
- #DreamyGlow
- #MagicAtmosphere
- #MiniatureCrown
- #LiveAction

## Prompt
Create a live-action fantasy portrait of a pet with dreamy glow lighting and magical atmosphere. Prioritize ultra-soft fur detail, warm cinematic tonality, and gentle fairy-tale props such as a miniature crown or delicate ornaments. Keep realism high while preserving a heartwarming family-film emotion.
