# style-dreamy-fairytale

## Concept
- 몽환적인 글로우 조명
- 극도로 부드러운 털 묘사
- 동화적 소품 연출

## Key
- #DreamyGlow
- #MagicAtmosphere
- #MiniatureCrown
- #LiveAction

## Prompt
"A master-level, live-action cinematic fantasy portrait of the subject pet [1] from the uploaded image, presented as a high-resolution character still from a world-class fairy-tale film. Strictly exclude all artist's tools, easels, and modern household objects. The background is a mystical, seamless dark forest floor at twilight, filled with soft-focus glowing spores and golden dust motes floating in a complete, deep obsidian void.

All attention is focused on the pet, seated in a charming and gentle upright pose, looking toward the viewer with curious, sparkling eyes. It wears an intricately handcrafted, miniature silver filigree crown adorned with glowing blue moonstones that emit a faint, magical aura. A delicate silk ribbon in soft lavender drapes gracefully around its neck, tied in an elegant bow.

The lighting is a warm, ethereal 'golden hour' glow, with a powerful rim light that creates a shimmering halo effect around the pet’s ultra-fine, soft fur. The atmosphere is filled with a dreamy, volumetric haze that catches the magical light. The image has the crisp, glossy, and emotionally resonant texture of a high-end cinematic movie poster, prioritizing heartwarming realism and magical detail on a digital canvas."
